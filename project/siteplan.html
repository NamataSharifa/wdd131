Site Name

Budget Bites

The name Budget Bites represents affordable food options for students. It is simple, memorable, and clearly communicates the purpose of the site.

Optional Domain: budgetbites.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help students find affordable meals, food deals, and budget-friendly restaurants near their campus. It provides pricing, locations, and student discounts.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find meals under R50 near me?
  • Which restaurants offer student discounts?
  • What are the cheapest fast food options available today?
  • Where can I get a filling meal on a small budget?
